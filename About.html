About Me

I’m a self-taught programmer who enjoys figuring out how things work and building useful tools with code. I’ve spent a lot of time digging into the core concepts of programming and pushing myself to learn by doing. This includes working on personal projects and exploring topics that challenge me. Lately, I’ve been especially focused on web development and artificial intelligence.

I have a strong interest in making things efficient, so I tend to focus on optimization and improving every project I work on, no matter the size or scope.
